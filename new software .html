💡 Best Practices

  • Combine Bonuses with Automation Sequences for greater effect.
  • Personalize the bonus messages to feel more human.
  • Use urgency (e.g., “valid for 24 hours only”) to increase conversions.
  • Send different bonuses for SMS vs WhatsApp (e.g., richer visuals for WhatsApp).
  • Regularly review your Bonus History to optimize performance.
  • Ensure your bonus redemption tracking is accurate (integrate with store if possible).
